User Guide

How to Join or Create a Study Group

1. Navigate to Groups

Click on the 'Groups' tab in the main navigation bar.

2. Search for Groups

Use the search bar to find groups by course name, topic, or keywords.

3. Join a Group

If you find a group that interests you, click on it to view details and join.

4. Create a Group

If no existing group fits your needs, click the 'Create Group' button.

5. Configure Group Settings

Fill out the group details, including name, description, course, and privacy settings.

6. Invite Members

Once created, invite classmates or share the group link.

Rules & Regulations

AcadNet is committed to fostering a respectful and collaborative learning environment. All users must adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Respectful Communication: Engage in constructive discussions and avoid offensive language.
  • Academic Integrity: Do not share or request unauthorized materials or engage in plagiarism.
  • Privacy: Respect the privacy of others and do not share personal information without consent.
  • Content Moderation: AcadNet reserves the right to remove inappropriate content and suspend accounts that violate these rules.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on 'Forgot Password'. Follow the instructions sent to your registered email address.

Can I join multiple study groups?

Yes, you can join as many study groups as you find relevant to your academic needs. There is no limit to the number of groups you can be a part of.

How do I report a user?

If you encounter a user violating platform rules, you can report them by navigating to their profile and clicking the 'Report User' button. Provide a brief description of the issue for our moderation team.
